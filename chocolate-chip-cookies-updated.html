Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Who doesn't love some chocolate chip cookies? In this recipe, you will learn how to make this famous dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees fan forced.
  2. Melt butter in microwave until melted.
  3. Combine flour and butter in a mixing bowl and mix thoroughly.
  4. Add eggs, baking powder and sugar and mix well.
  5. Add chocolate chips and mix with hands until distributed evenly.
  6. Using your hands, roll dough into palm sized balls and place onto a baking tray, sprayed with cooking oil or with baking paper on it.
  7. Once all the batter has been rolled, place the tray into the oven.
  8. Cook for 18 minutes, or until desired texture has been achieved.
  9. You have now made chocolate chip cookies!
