Banana Pancakes

Home

Description

Banana pancakes are a classic dish - perfect for both breakfast and dessert. On this webpage, you will learn how to make these delicious pancakes yourself!

Full of protein and all the great vitamins and minerals bananas provide, you won't regret making this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk of your choice
  • 1/2 cup vanilla protein powder
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • Sugar free maple syrup to taste
  • Vanilla extract to taste
  • Two eggs
  • Two bananas

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs thoroughly, until bubbly.
  2. Mash the bananas until there are no lumps left.
  3. Mix the whisked eggs and bananas in a bowl until mixed well.
  4. Add flour and mix thoroughly.
  5. Add milk and mix thoroughly.
  6. Add protein powder and mix well.
  7. Add cinnamon, vanilla extract and sugar free maple syrup to taste.
  8. After mixing all the ingredients together, you are now ready to start cooking your pancakes!
  9. Measure 1/2 cup of the pancake mix, and add to a pan on low-medium heat.
  10. When the pancake begins bubbling around the edges, flip the pancake and repeat on the other side.
  11. Cook for approximately 3 minutes.
  12. Optionally, you may add other ingredients whilst the pancake is cooking, such as chocolate chips or berries.
  13. Remove the pancake from the pan onto a plate, and decorate/add other ingredients as desired.
  14. You have now made banana pancakes!
